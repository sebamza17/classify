Image ratio helpers

These helper classes will aid you when in need of maintaining a certain aspect ratio on your images. It takes advantages of the :before pseudoelement and background-size: cover to make an image maintain its proportions

This image will always maintain a 16:9 aspect ratio. Use this range control to change the container width.

Image ratio variations

Change this range input to see how other image ratios behave on container width changes.

.image-ratio-1-1;
.image-ratio-4-3;
